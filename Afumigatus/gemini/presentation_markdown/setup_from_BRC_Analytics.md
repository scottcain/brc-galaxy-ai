# How to Analyze Aspergillus fumigatus Variants in BRC Analytics
#### [Made by Scott Cain with Scribe](https://scribehow.com/shared/How_to_Analyze_Aspergillus_fumigatus_Variants_in_BRC_Analytics__PQoKcDb9S6yhyvBOSjLXpg)
This guide demonstrates how to analyze variant calls for Aspergillus fumigatus in the BRC Analytics platform. Follow these steps to access organism data, configure variant calling inputs, and launch your analysis in Galaxy.

1\. Navigate to [https://brc-analytics.org/](https://brc-analytics.org/)

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-03-05/ecac894a-7592-4741-a140-aec6c353e5ac/ascreenshot_630bee7ebef34bec8a8f7902f0259449_text_export.jpeg)


2\. Click "Organisms"

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-03-05/ecac894a-7592-4741-a140-aec6c353e5ac/ascreenshot_47a380b477de4179b6784a3e1a202978_text_export.jpeg)


3\. Click "Filter"

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-03-05/5824dc0b-63eb-4410-953e-a40822797f0b/ascreenshot_fb29afa76a8c4e768c76996a1dff082b_text_export.jpeg)


4\. Type "a"


5\. Click the "Search all filters..." field.

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-03-05/8ab545b2-14f7-4001-aaa8-09a121f926ad/ascreenshot_90a55d99ea5b4c7db681109691ad8642_text_export.jpeg)


6\. Type "asp"


7\. Click this checkbox.

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-03-05/12051fb0-b42c-4768-a13b-7d71b1cdbe83/ascreenshot_2c2d5071dd2643bb80f2b9424a894be8_text_export.jpeg)


8\. Click here.

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-03-05/a2a6a4fa-b37d-4319-9fd6-b3a5882c3510/ascreenshot_4afce5a85b8a4f37801976565183470b_text_export.jpeg)


9\. Right-click "Aspergillus fumigatus"

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-03-05/73e28529-a164-461b-8d65-8aad8228b7e3/ascreenshot_2eeadbcf24a34f1e8124d757429d9074_text_export.jpeg)


10\. Click "Aspergillus fumigatus"

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-03-05/4ae4f3c5-8073-4d42-b6c4-0c2e8148df2c/ascreenshot_19f74a371bdf4fb7a5e57b569afb42d4_text_export.jpeg)


11\. Click "Analyze"

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-03-05/189e663f-3b66-48cb-a2b4-0a5ed7c1574c/ascreenshot_ed6548160ee84d64b802bb1a4bd4c5e2_text_export.jpeg)


12\. Click "Variant calling"

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-03-05/c4541f7e-aed0-49b1-92e0-e7ae52a54a90/ascreenshot_266be7742b1245c3b31adde71673526c_text_export.jpeg)


13\. Click "Configure Inputs"

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-03-05/003f23af-2b58-40d6-979d-a84cbb4f5103/ascreenshot_8d09772562574bcebc588011537de73e_text_export.jpeg)


14\. Click "Continue"

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-03-05/2f62a515-f544-44ac-92fa-d38205f4f13d/ascreenshot_498c2fbb8d1d481089f777f60ac4ce58_text_export.jpeg)


15\. Click "Enter Accession(s)"

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-03-05/29b05dc3-40ac-42fd-a632-2ae22d3c3009/ascreenshot_cb1f99aaa0cc42e69c60298d8a2f2a78_text_export.jpeg)


16\. Click this text field.

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-03-05/ff389824-5d91-42d5-bb6e-3395df22ef2a/ascreenshot_19a8e230bc3940a091c6bb5d824f6459_text_export.jpeg)


17\. Press [[cmd]] + [[v]]

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-03-05/324e9213-e4fa-4929-874f-90ae1804be1e/ascreenshot_c4773441da524ba1a374a4c0b7603557_text_export.jpeg)


18\. Click "Continue"

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-03-05/79397e53-ce44-4d45-8153-2ee5ad82c1bb/ascreenshot_dbe24630016543a48b354dea98a6d92a_text_export.jpeg)


19\. Click this checkbox.

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-03-05/a122b60c-683b-40ff-a8b1-e217cc111f4e/ascreenshot_eab908ff2b0e4f0e945a9d58f576e5aa_text_export.jpeg)


20\. Click "Add 22 Sequencing Runs"

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-03-05/23de7d51-0bd8-4344-b092-5a1624f6ebf0/ascreenshot_a3c7b6e8bf9743e1ac3c10d0d9885859_text_export.jpeg)
